Our Recipes

Here are some of our favorite strawberry lemonade recipes:

  • Classic Strawberry Lemonade

    Ingredients:

    • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
    • 1/2 cup mashed strawberries
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 4 cups cold water
    • Ice cubes
    Instructions:
    1. In a pitcher, combine the lemon juice, mashed strawberries, sugar, and water. Stir well until the sugar is dissolved.
    2. Add ice cubes to serving glasses and pour the strawberry lemonade over the ice.
    3. Garnish with fresh strawberry slices and serve chilled.

  • Strawberry Lemonade with Basil

    Ingredients:

    • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
    • 1/2 cup mashed strawberries
    • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 4 cups cold water
    • Ice cubes
    Instructions:
    1. In a blender, combine the lemon juice, mashed strawberries, fresh basil leaves, sugar, and water. Blend until smooth.
    2. Add ice cubes to serving glasses and pour the strawberry basil lemonade over the ice.
    3. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve chilled.

  • Sparkling Strawberry Lemonade

    Ingredients:

    • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
    • 1/2 cup mashed strawberries
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 4 cups sparkling water
    • Ice cubes
    Instructions:
    1. In a pitcher, combine the lemon juice, mashed strawberries, sugar, and sparkling water. Stir well until the sugar is dissolved.
    2. Add ice cubes to serving glasses and pour the sparkling strawberry lemonade over the ice.
    3. Garnish with fresh strawberry slices and serve chilled.
